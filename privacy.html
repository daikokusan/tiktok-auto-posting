Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Summary: TikTok Auto Posting is a local automation tool. It does not collect, store, or share your personal data with any third parties beyond TikTok's official API. All data stays on your device.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how TikTok Auto Posting ("the App") handles information when you use it to generate and publish content to TikTok. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

Data TypeStorage LocationPurpose
TikTok OAuth Access TokenLocal .env fileAuthenticate API requests to TikTok
Generated video filesLocal output/videos/ folderStore generated content before publishing
Publication historyLocal SQLite database (tiktok.db)Track published posts and analytics
System logsLocal logs/ folderDebugging and error tracking

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect or transmit:

  • Your TikTok username, email, or profile information
  • Your TikTok followers list or following list
  • Your device identifiers or location data
  • Any analytics or usage data to external servers
  • Cookies or tracking data of any kind

4. How We Use TikTok API

The App communicates with TikTok's official API endpoints only for the following purposes:

  • OAuth Authentication - To obtain an access token via TikTok's Login Kit (OAuth 2.0 PKCE flow)
  • Video Upload - To upload video files to TikTok's servers using the video.upload scope
  • Video Publish - To publish uploaded videos to your TikTok account using the video.publish scope

All API communications are made directly to open.tiktokapis.com. No data is routed through intermediate servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. The only external service the App communicates with is TikTok's official API. TikTok's own privacy practices are governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Security

Your OAuth access token is stored in a local .env file on your device. You are responsible for ensuring that file is protected from unauthorized access. Tokens expire automatically after 24 hours and must be refreshed.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Revoke access: You can revoke the App's TikTok authorization at any time via TikTok → Settings & Privacy → Security → Authorized Apps
  • Delete local data: You can delete any locally stored files (videos, database, logs) directly from your device
  • Stop the app: You can disable the automated scheduler at any time by unloading the launchd agents

8. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please refer to the App homepage.
